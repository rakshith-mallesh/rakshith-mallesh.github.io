About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in Climate Dynamics in the Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences at the University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Dev Niyogi. Before UT, I was at the Indian Institute of Science, studying the decadal variability of monsoons, advised by Prof. Jayaraman Srinivasan. In my research, I tend to focus on studying the physics of monsoons, the Inter Tropical Convergence Zone (ITCZ), and tropical climate in general. I am especially interested in these areas in the context of climate change. For my PhD, I will be looking at hurricanes. My email is: rakshith@utexas.edu
